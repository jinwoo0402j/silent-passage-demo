# Background Style Target

## Locked Direction

Use this direction for Silent Passage flooded-city, shelter, apartment-window, and home-base background art.

The target is not raw photorealism and not full anime. It is:

```text
Sun-washed pearl-gray realistic anime background painting.
```

Preserve realistic architecture, lighting, camera perspective, and spatial depth, then treat the color grading, air, and surface texture like a high-quality theatrical animated-film background painting.

## Core Feeling

- Realistic apartment interiors, window frames, blinds, balcony railings, floodwater, and concrete towers.
- Warm post-rain sunlight and calm water reflections.
- Bright pearl-gray concrete instead of dark gray ruin tones.
- Pale green vines, young leaves, rooftop shrubs, and reclaimed vegetation.
- Quiet, beautiful, hopeful post-apocalypse.
- Soft cinematic haze and organized animated-film color grading.
- Subtle painterly edges and light brush texture.
- Readable foreground shapes for game use.

## Avoid

- Raw, ungraded photorealistic photography.
- Full oil painting or thick impasto that obscures architecture.
- Cartoonish anime line art or hard black outlines.
- Dark gray dystopia, horror, cyberpunk, or strong neon.
- Strong contrast, heavy dirt, gore, or disgusting ruin detail.
- Oversaturated green or fantasy-jungle overgrowth.
- Cluttered shelter rooms that leave no space for character sprites or dialogue UI.

## Reusable Prompt Core

```text
Realistic architecture, lighting, and spatial depth, rendered with the color grading and soft texture of a high-quality animated film background painting. Sun-washed pearl-gray concrete, warm sunlight, calm water reflections, pale green vines and vegetation, quiet hopeful post-apocalypse. Between photorealism and anime background art: cinematic realism, soft painterly edges, subtle brush texture, no hard outlines, not raw photoreal, not full oil painting.
```

## Shelter/Home Composition Notes

- Use 16:9 landscape compositions for CG and home-base backgrounds.
- Leave a calm open foreground or side area when a Type-07A sprite or dialogue UI may be overlaid.
- Keep props understated and low along the edges: folded blanket, low table, crate, cup, tiny cyan device light.
- Let windows, blinds, balcony rails, floodwater, and pale apartment towers carry the scene.
- Use vegetation as light and atmosphere, not as chaotic clutter.
