# Face-off Character Style Prompt

## Confirmed Style

```text
Stylized indie cartoon character illustration, NOT SD/chibi.
Proportions around 4.5 to 5 heads tall, angular simplified anatomy, large expressive head but not super-deformed, sharp shoulders and long torso.
Thick dark navy/black outlines, flat cel shading, minimal gradients, simple graphic shadows.
Limited muted palette: black, charcoal, white/silver hair, pale skin, muted red accents, occasional small cyan tech accents.
Expression style: narrow red eyes, sly confident smirk, mischievous but dangerous attitude.
Clean sticker-like silhouette on a pure black or transparent-feeling background.
Avoid realistic painting, highly detailed anime render, soft painterly rendering, tiny chibi body, UI, text, watermark.
```

## Face-off Enemy Add-on

```text
Apply this style to the Face-off enemy: long silver/white hair, black tactical bodysuit/armor, translucent white-gray coat panels, small tech accents, rifle held casually at her side, relaxed threatening three-quarter pose.
```
